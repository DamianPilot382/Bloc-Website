AV Workshop

Last september, the varsity virtual business company, Bloc, sent several representatives to conduct a workshop for the students of Arroyo Vista Elementary School. The purpose of the workshop was to instruct the students on creating their own companies. The students then used what they learned from the workshop to design their companies that they later debuted at a school entrepreneur fair.

Arroyo Vista Elementary Workshop, 2017

Parent Night

In February, the members of Bloc invited their families for our annual parent night. It began with a introduction from the president of the Virtual Business Club, Ben Elbaum. Ben discussed our upcoming competitions and the virtual business program. Then our CEO, Kate Ba, introduced our company. Students then presented their competition pieces. This included an elevator pitch, marketing plan, and business plan. Overall, the reactions of the parents were very positive and it was a successful evening.
