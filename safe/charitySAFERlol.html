Habitat for Humanity is a global nonprofit housing organization working in nearly 1,400 communities across the United States and around the World. Their vision is of a world where everyone has a decent place to live.


Bloc’s goal in working with Habitat will be to assist low-income families to find affordable housing. We will donate our blocks, modular furniture pieces, to Habitat for Humanity’s ReStores. These ReStores sell furniture, houseware, building materials, etc. at a highly discounted price, donating the proceeds to help build homes. We will also donate our time by volunteering at both the ReStores and the home building sites of Habitat for Humanity. We hope our donation of blocs and time will promote the value we associate with creating something brand new using your own creativity.


By establishing this program in support of Habitat for Humanity, Bloc can provide low-income families in the San Gabriel Valley with affordable housing and the chance to lead a better life.
