About Us

At Bloc, we focus on giving our customers a creative alternative to modern day furniture. Our customizable modular furniture consists of varying sizes of blocks made from recycled plastic. Our consumers are encouraged to exercise their creative ability by constantly modifying their designs. Whether a person is designing or redesigning their space, we provide the tools to do so. We also exhibit our social responsibility through our partnership with Habitat for Humanity. Bloc is able to assist those whose lives have been affected by natural disasters or poverty by rebuilding their lives through rebuilding their homes.
